WHO WE ARE

The Engineering Investment Group (EIG) was founded in 2012 by engineering students at McGill University. We are currently one of the most significant clubs in the engineering faculty and expanding every year. EIG aims to bridge the gap between engineering and finance by providing opportunities for engineering students to learn about the ever changing world of finance. We educate engineering students through real-life investment activities and events. We want to create a community of finance-oriented individuals to share their thoughts and challenge each other’s ideas. The objectives of EIG are to showcase the merit of McGill Engineering students to large financial institutions and for students to gain and demonstrate investment skills and knowledge.

Attendees:

  • Professionals and students from multiple universities in Montreal, with an engineering background, interested in finance.
  • Professionals and students from multiple universities in Montreal, with a finance background.
